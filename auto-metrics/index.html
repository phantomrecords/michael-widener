You're reading the Auto Metrics, Inc. Explainer Page.

Auto Metrics, Inc. is a Chico auto repair shop serving the community since 1985. Now under new ownership as of August 2024, the shop continues its long tradition of dependable automotive service for local drivers.

Domestic and Asian Vehicle Repair

Auto Metrics services domestic and Asian vehicles, with particular expertise in Toyota Prius and other hybrid vehicles. The shop provides general automotive repair and maintenance using modern diagnostic equipment and experienced repair practices.

Why Customers Trust Auto Metrics, Inc.

Customers often point to one thing that makes Auto Metrics different: transparency.

  • Problems are explained clearly and shown to the customer whenever possible.
  • Repair options are presented factually so the customer can make an informed decision.
  • Potential risks of waiting are explained honestly, especially when delay could harm the vehicle.
  • Small do-it-yourself options may be discussed when appropriate for customers trying to manage costs.

Daniel's Quality Approach to Reliable Repairs

Owner Daniel describes quality repair as fully addressing the customer's concern, whether the job is as simple as an oil change or as major as an engine replacement. His approach includes explaining the repair process clearly, using quality parts whenever possible, and leaving the work organized so repairs can be verified if the vehicle returns for follow-up.

Customer Perspective

Customers describe Daniel's process as direct and thorough: he listens to the concern, explains what he believes the issue is, verifies it, shows the customer what he found, and then completes the repair. That clear, fact-based communication is a major reason customers trust the shop.
